What is ACTS?

ACTS ATX is an Austin-based organization (seeking non-profit 501c3) status formed by two UT graduates for the sole purpose of bringing communities closer together. Acts ATX focuses on Acts of Service that benefit the immediate low income community, such as: Volunteering, Clothes and Kindness, Project Educate and Counseling*

*Due to Covid, our programs are only offered via virtual sessions only.

Volunteering

Connecting individuals and building social networks through volunteering in the Austin community.

Project Donation

Collecting lightly-worn clothing, school supplies from willing donors and businesses, refurbishing them and sending them to families in need.

Project Educate

Provide education to children in need via customized tutoring sessions (ESL, Math, Korean, Computer Programming, Research Theory and Writing).

Counseling

Connecting individuals seeking academic counseling and counseling for mental health needs.
